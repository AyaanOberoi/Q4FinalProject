Weighted priorities

What earns the A band

Completion is gates; insight, sourcing, visuals, facilitation, rehearsal earn the spotlight.

30%

Completeness

All seven prompts satisfied with distinct slide intent + visible labels.

25%

Systems thinking

US economy lens, evolving policy/regulation, global fallout, classmates’ lived stakes.

20%

Evidence packs

Primary reporting + MLA stack; charts anchored to authoritative stats.

15%

Visual narration

Diagram-heavy storytelling—not stock templates with serif bullet spam.

10%

Delivery readiness

Timing, neutrality, facilitation of discussion, preparedness for sensitivities.

Slide playbook

Copy this checklist verbatim

Each slide is a scene—assign an icon motif, unify colors, cite sources on-slide when showing data cuts.

01

Headline slate

Your name, article title, outlet, reporter, publish date.

02

Neutral summary

Actors, timelines, financial magnitude, disputed vs confirmed facts.

03

Historical lens

Firm dossier—or macro history if lacking a protagonist company.

04

Impact matrix

United States economy • regulations/laws/policy • globe • classmates.

05

Curriculum tether

Teach unfamiliar concepts if branching beyond class vocabulary.

06

Facilitation toolkit

2–3 open prompts that force compare/contrast/speculation ethically.

07

Works cited · MLA

List article, supporting reads, statistic portals, infographic credits.

Visual upgrades that pop

Scenario trees, quadrant maps for stakeholders, infographic timelines, choropleths, annotated sparkline clusters, icon-driven supply chains, before/after unit economics dashboards.

Data guardrails

Blend multiple dataset families—survey, CPI, employment, tariff schedules, capex trackers. Stock ticker alone ≠ compliance.

Sensitive topics workflow

Book time with instructor to frame trauma-informed language and classroom norms before designing visuals.
